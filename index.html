This is my attempt to calculate my personal environmental footprint as accurately as I know how.

I try to live as simply and sustainably as I can, and have been acquiring this data for years because it is something I find interesting. I am currently in the process of converting all of this data into something that can be updated in real-time via this website. This is a work in progress; it will get better slowly. HTML-only currently.

Climate change is heavily impacted because of human-activities. If you believe otherwise, you are wrong.
